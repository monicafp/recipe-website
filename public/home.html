Rustic Key Lime Tarts

Colaborador(a): Mónica Fornaris Pau
Source: Idle Wife

Ingredientes

Crust:
1 1/2 cup honey graham cracker crumbs
1/4 cup sugar
zest from one lime
90 mls or 6 tablespoons salted margarine, melted

Filling:
2 egg yolks
1 can fat free/low fat sweetened condensed milk
1/2 cup key lime juice (the equivalent of exactly one pound of key limes)

Procedimiento

Crust:
1. Crush the zest throughout the sugar.
2. Combine the crumbs, sugar, and lime zest
3. Mix in the melted butter until everything is evenly moist.
4. Butter a 12 cup nonsick muffin tin.
4. Take the crumbs and divide them as equally as you can into the muffin tin.
5. Using a cup that approximately fits the bottom of the tin, push the crumbs down to the bottom. Kind of wiggle the cup around so the crumbs go up the sides a bit. Get your fingers involved if you need to.
6. Bake in a 350 degree oven for about 10 minutes (mine puffed up and freaked me out, but they settled back down when they came out). Allow to cool.

Filling:
1. Mix the condensed milk and egg yolks together until combined. Add the lime juice and stir to combine. It will thicken up almost instantly.
2. Pour about 1/2 cup of batter into each graham cracker cup.
3. Cook at 350 for 15 minutes.
4. Cool and chill in the fridge for about an hour.
